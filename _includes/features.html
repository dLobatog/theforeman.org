CLI

UIs slow you down? Hammer CLI gives you easy access to all the API calls you need to stay on top of your data center.

Discovery

Unknown nodes on your provisioning network? Discovery will find them and register them in Foreman. You can define rules to decide what to do with discovered nodes.

RBAC and LDAP integration

A thorough authorization system based on role based access control (RBAC) allows for tight policies for your Foreman users. If you use LDAP, or IPA, you can continue using them for authentication and authorization.

Host groups

Horizontal scaling is an ubiquitous architecture in IT. Group your hosts in groups, and modify their configuration as if they were one with Foreman.

Plugins

A pluggable architecture allows you to extend Foreman in almost any direction. Visit our list of plugins for a glimpse.

Audits

No more wondering why did your load balancer suddenly became a database. Check how, who and when in our audits system.
